What is TED-Ed Student Talk?

TED is a nonprofit organization devoted to spreading ideas, usually in the form of short, powerful talks.

How it works

1
Stage 1

TED-Ed approves the hosting of a student talks event.

2
Stage 2

Preparing the students for the event.

3
Stage 3

Hosting a live event and filming the talks for Ted-ed Club.
